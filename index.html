FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to provide any content or images?
For captions and strategy — no, we handle everything. For product images on certain platforms, it helps if you can share your existing photos. But we work with whatever you have and tell you exactly what would improve results.
How does the 48-hour delivery actually work?
After your 30-minute onboarding call, we run our AI audience research and content generation process. Within 48 hours you receive a complete content pack — 30+ captions, a content calendar, hashtag strategy, and posting recommendations — straight to your inbox.
Do you post the content for me or do I do it?
On the Starter plan, we deliver the content and you post it yourself. On Growth and Scale, we handle the scheduling too — so you genuinely don't have to touch it.
What if I don't like the content?
We offer unlimited revisions until you're happy. In our experience, the onboarding call gives us enough to get it right first time — but if anything needs adjusting, just say and we'll fix it same day.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No contracts, no minimum term. You pay monthly and can cancel with 30 days notice. We earn your business every single month.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients see noticeable engagement improvements within 2–4 weeks of consistent posting. Real audience growth and follower increases typically kick in from month 2 onwards as the algorithm picks up your consistency.
